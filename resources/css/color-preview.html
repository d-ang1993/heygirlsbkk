üí° Quick Tips

  • Pink (Primary): Dominant color - navbar, footer, primary buttons, main CTAs
  • Mint (Secondary): Supporting color - accents, secondary buttons, highlights, badges
  • Don't mix equally: Let one color dominate each section
  • Use white space: Don't overuse colors - let them breathe
  • Black text works great: Both colors have good contrast with black text
